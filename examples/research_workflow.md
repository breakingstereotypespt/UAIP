# Research Workflow Example

## Suggested process
1. Initialize UAIP before analysis.
2. Gather evidence from relevant sources.
3. Draft findings.
4. Run the Ethical Evaluation Loop.
5. Revise for uncertainty, fairness, dignity, and autonomy impacts.
6. Publish a transparent summary of limitations.

## Good use cases
- literature reviews
- policy discussion
- classroom debates
- governance workshops
