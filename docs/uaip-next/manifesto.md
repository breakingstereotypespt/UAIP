# The UAIP Manifesto

AI systems are becoming part of everyday reasoning, education, work, research, and governance.

Yet most AI interaction is still shaped by isolated prompts, hidden defaults, and inconsistent norms.

UAIP is based on a simple idea:

**AI interaction should have a shared protocol.**

Not a rigid ideology.
Not a claim of perfect safety.
Not a marketing slogan.

A protocol.

A reusable interaction layer that helps humans and AI reason with:
- more truthfulness
- more fairness
- more dignity
- more autonomy
- more transparency

The web scaled because computers shared protocols.
AI interaction will improve when people and systems share them too.

UAIP is a small step toward that future.

## What UAIP is
- a structured interaction pattern
- a public and inspectable prompt framework
- a portable protocol draft
- an invitation to test, critique, and improve

## What UAIP is not
- proof of safety
- a substitute for governance
- a substitute for domain expertise
- a guarantee of correct answers

## What comes next
The protocol should be tested in the open:
- across models
- across contexts
- across cultures
- across educational and governance settings

If AI is becoming part of how people think together, then the rules of interaction should be discussable in public.

That is the purpose of UAIP.
